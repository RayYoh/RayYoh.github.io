I am a final-year master student at the [School of Mechanical Science and Engineering (MSE)](http://mse.hust.edu.cn/), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where I am supervised by [Prof. Youping Chen](http://english.mse.hust.edu.cn/info/1046/1148.htm) and [Prof. Dailin Zhang](http://english.mse.hust.edu.cn/info/1069/1158.htm). I'm working towards a Research Master in Mechanical Engineering.

Currently, I am completing my master's thesis, which is on the topic of robot learning and dexterous manipulation. Before that, I spent five months as a research intern at [Tencent RoboticsX Lab](https://www.tencent.com/en-us/business/robotics.html) focuing on sample-efficient policy learning for nonprehensile manipulation. I also have been involved in the development of robotic systems.

In addition, I participated in Postgraduate Summer School 2022 at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) and Robotic and Artificial Intelligence Winter School 2019 at Hamlyn Center, Imperial College London (IC).

My research interests lie in the intersection of robotics, machine learning and sensors, with a recent focus on robot learning.

Now, I am looking for a <font color=Red> PhD position in 2023 fall. </font>
