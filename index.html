    Research

    My current research interests lie in the intersection of 3D Vision and Visual Representation for Embodied AI, with particular interests in 3D scene understanding, sample-efficient and generalizable policy learning. My research objective is to bulid an embodied agent which has the ability to efficiently perceive and interact with complex real-world environment. Previously, I have worked on robot Compliance control and Nonprehensile manipulation.
