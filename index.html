    Research

    My current research interests lie in the intersection of Conputer Vision and Robotics, with particular interests in representation learning, object perception and understanding. My research objective is to bulid an embodied agent to efficiently perceive and interact with complex real-world environment. Previously, I have worked on Compliance control and Nonprehensile manipulation.
